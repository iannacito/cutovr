Disconnect QuickBooks

This page is the official Disconnect URL registered with Intuit for {{ app_name }}. You can land here from QuickBooks → Apps → Connected apps, or by clicking the link directly.

Two ways to disconnect

  1. From QuickBooks Online: sign in to QuickBooks, open Apps → My Apps (or Settings → Apps and integrations) and click Disconnect next to {{ app_name }}. This revokes the app's access at Intuit. We recommend doing this and the in-app disconnect below for a clean removal.
  2. From {{ app_name }}: sign in to your firm workspace, then click Disconnect below to revoke the stored OAuth tokens server-side. The encrypted tokens are deleted immediately. Journal entries already posted to QuickBooks are not deleted — they remain in your QuickBooks company until you reverse or remove them there.

What disconnecting does not do

  • It does not delete or reverse any JournalEntry records {{ app_name }} already posted to QuickBooks. Those stay in your QuickBooks company until you reverse them in {{ app_name }} or delete them in QuickBooks.
  • It does not remove your firm workspace, uploaded files, or import history from {{ app_name }}.
  • It does not remove duplicate-import protection. If you reconnect later and try to upload the same file to the same QuickBooks company, the duplicate guard still blocks it.
