What we move

A firm’s books are more than one file. We handle the records that actually have to come across.

Chart of accounts

Matched to your QuickBooks accounts with a side-by-side preview before anything is created.

Trial balances

Parsed and totaled to seed opening balances and to verify the books later.

General ledgers

Reviewed, then posted as journal entries — not dumped in blind.

Vendor & customer lists
