Private beta — please read before importing

Do not upload a full client ledger on your first run. Export a small slice of the GL (one or two transactions), import it, and confirm the journal entries land cleanly in QuickBooks before sending the full file. Always review the Map accounts screen before posting — a wrong mapping will write to the wrong QuickBooks account and has to be reversed afterward. See the full onboarding guide.
