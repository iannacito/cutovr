Quick answers

  • QuickBooks rejected the import. Open your migration, go to Match accounts, and make sure every PCLaw account is matched to a QuickBooks account. Then try sending again.
  • I imported into the wrong company. Open the migration, scroll to Reverse this import, type REVERSE, and click the button. QuickBooks will receive offsetting entries; the originals stay visible for audit.
  • “Duplicate import blocked.” The same file was already sent to this QuickBooks company. Either export a fresh ledger from PCLaw covering only the new period, or remove the prior entries in QuickBooks before re-sending.
  • Reversal entries don't say “reversal” in QuickBooks. Reversals posted by {{ app_name }} have a document number starting REV- and every line description prefixed REVERSAL. The QuickBooks Journal report shows them as separate entries alongside the originals; QuickBooks does not visually mark the originals as voided.

Reporting an issue

Email {{ support_email }}. Please include:

  • The migration name or reference shown at the top of the page.
  • The exact action you took (button or page).
  • The error message text you saw, verbatim.
  • The approximate time you saw the issue.
  • Whether you were testing with sample data or running a real migration.

Do not paste passwords, OAuth tokens, or full PCLaw ledger content into a support email. Your migration reference is enough for us to look it up.

Reporting a security issue

If you think you've found a security problem (login bypass, leaked data, etc.), please email {{ security_email }} instead of posting publicly. Include the same details as above plus a short reproduction. We will acknowledge within 2 business days.

Status & uptime

If the application appears down, our hosting provider (Render) restarts it automatically. If problems persist for more than a few minutes, email {{ support_email }} and we'll investigate.

Disconnecting QuickBooks

To disconnect QuickBooks from {{ app_name }}, open the Disconnect QuickBooks page. From there a logged-in firm admin can revoke access and remove the stored connection. You can also reach the same page from QuickBooks Online → Apps → Connected apps → Disconnect.
