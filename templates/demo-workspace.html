Rehearsed demo path

Follow this exact order on a live demo — don't click around. The first two steps are prep on this page; steps 1–6 are the customer-facing workflow the prospect sees, each with a single primary button that takes you to the next step.

  1. Prep A. Click Start a new demo above to reset the app workspace to a clean slate.
  2. Prep B. Confirm the right QuickBooks demo company is connected (see panel above), then download the four sample reports below.
  3. Step 1 — Set up: open Set up your switchover, enter the cutover date and country, save.
  4. Step 2 — Upload: on the dashboard, upload the Chart of Accounts, Trial Balance, and General Ledger sample files.
  5. Step 3 — Match accounts: follow the single primary button to match accounts; create any missing QuickBooks accounts when prompted.
  6. Step 4 — Review: open the review screen and confirm everything balances.
  7. Step 5 — Send to QuickBooks: use Send to QuickBooks to post the migration.
  8. Step 6 — Final balance check: open Reconcile balances and show the final report (and email it to yourself).

The on-screen stepper always highlights the current step and shows one next button on the right and a back button on the left, so you can drive the whole demo from those buttons without hunting through the nav.
