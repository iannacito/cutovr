Pricing

Why we quote on the call

No two firms migrate the same way.

A few things change the shape — and cost — of a move. We’d rather review them with you than guess up front.

Report quality

How clean and complete your PCLaw exports are decides how much review and refinement a migration needs.

Migration history

How many years of history you want carried into QuickBooks changes the volume of work.

Trust accounting

Firms that hold client trust funds need careful reconciliation — the most sensitive part of any law-firm move.

Because data complexity varies firm to firm, we scope your migration on the discovery call and provide a clear quote on the call — reviewed by our team.

Questions

Plain English answers.

How much does a migration cost?

It depends on your reports, how much history you’re moving, and how your trust accounting is kept. We scope it on the call and quote it there, before any work begins.

What happens on the discovery call?

We review what you’re moving out of PCLaw, talk through any trust or reconciliation needs, and confirm scope. Then we give you a quote for the migration, right on the call.

Does this include QuickBooks setup?

You'll need an active QuickBooks Online subscription. Once it’s active, we handle connecting it to {{ app_name }}, matching your accounts, and posting your history. We don't sell QuickBooks itself.

Do I need to know which reports to upload?

No. We walk you through which PCLaw reports help us most. If something is missing or looks off, we flag it before anything is sent to QuickBooks.
