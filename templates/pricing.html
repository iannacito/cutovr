Pricing

Your quote comes from the discovery call.

{{ app_name }} is a done-for-you migration, so we price each one after we’ve scoped it with you on a short discovery call — not from a fixed menu. You leave the call knowing exactly what your migration costs.

Why we quote on the call

No two firms migrate the same way.

A few things change the shape — and the cost — of a migration. We’d rather look at them with you than guess at a number up front.

Report quality

How clean and complete your PCLaw exports are decides how much review and refinement a migration needs.

Migration history

How many years of history you want carried into QuickBooks changes the volume of work.

Trust accounting

Firms that hold client trust funds need careful reconciliation — the most sensitive part of any law-firm move.

Because data complexity varies firm to firm, we scope your migration on the discovery call and give you a quote right then — reviewed and refined by our team, with no surprise invoice afterward.

Questions

Plain English answers.

How much does a migration cost?

It depends on your reports, how much history you want moved, and how your trust accounting is kept. We scope your migration on the discovery call and give you a quote on that call — so you know the cost before any work begins.

What happens on the discovery call?

We review what you’re moving out of PCLaw, talk through any trust or reconciliation needs, and confirm scope. Then we give you a quote for the migration, right on the call.

Does this include QuickBooks setup?

You'll need an active QuickBooks Online subscription. Once you do, we handle connecting it to {{ app_name }}, matching your accounts, and posting your history. We don't sell QuickBooks itself.

Do I need to know which reports to upload?

No. The Calendly booking form and the discovery call walk you through exactly which PCLaw reports help us most. If something is missing or looks off, we flag it before anything is sent to QuickBooks.
