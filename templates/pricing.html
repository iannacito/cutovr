Questions

Plain English answers.

What if I am not sure how far back to go?

Most firms pick 3-Year History because it gives you enough context to feel confident in QuickBooks without dragging in ancient records. If you change your mind later, you can add extra years as an add-on.

Do I need to know which reports to upload?

No. The app tells you which files it wants and walks you through each one. If something is missing or looks off, we flag it before anything is sent to QuickBooks.

Does this include QuickBooks setup?

You'll need an active QuickBooks Online subscription. Once you do, we handle connecting it to PCLaw Migrate, matching your accounts, and posting your history. We don't sell QuickBooks itself.

What happens after I pay?

You get immediate access to the guided migration workflow. Upload your files, review what we found, connect QuickBooks, and send when you're ready. If anything looks wrong, the migration can be reversed in one click.
